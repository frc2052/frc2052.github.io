Knightkrawler STEMinar Series


STEMinar Series

KnightKrawler's STEMinar series aims to connect Irondale High School students interested in pursuing STEM fields and provide exposure to diverse role models, fields of study, and careers.


Seminars will be held virtually on Fridays (Learning Plus Days) at 1:30pm CT via Google Meet. Each week there will be a new speaker talking about their relationship with STEM.


December 4

Shehrzad Bazrafshan

Shehrzad lives in Minneapolis and works as a Design Electrical Engineer designing hand controls at Sensata Technologies.






December 11

Dr. Derya Aksaray

Derya Aksaray is an assistant professor of Aerospace Engineering and Mechanics at the University of Minnesota. Before joining the U of M, Aksaray has held post-doctoral positions at Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL), MIT from 2016-2017, and BU Robotics Lab, Boston University from 2014-2016.
