Knightkrawler STEMinar Series


STEMinar Series

KnightKrawler's STEMinar series aims to connect Irondale High School students interested in pursuing STEM fields and provide exposure to diverse role models, fields of study, and careers.


Seminars will be held virtually on Fridays (Learning Plus Days) at 1:30pm CT via Google Meet. Each week there will be a new speaker talking about their relationship with STEM.


January 8

Sean Dalton

Sean received his B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Minnesota in 2017 and is a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University since 2019. Prior to graduate school, Sean worked at Los Alamos National Laboratory studying shock driven flows and their instabilities. His specialization is in fluid phenomena, particularly turbulent flows.
