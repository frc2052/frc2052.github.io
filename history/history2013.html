This game starts off with a 15 second autonomous period where robots are controlled autonomously. Robots may control 11 inch Discs and score them in goals on the opponent’s side of the field. There are three goals above the opposing alliance’s drivers stations, the middle one worth one point more than the other two, another goal on one side of those drivers stations, and the last goal at the top of that alliance’s Pyramid. In Endgame, robots score by climbing their Pyramid and are rewarded points depending on which rung they climb on.
