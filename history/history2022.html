Team History

2022: Rapid React

Firefly

Robot Information

  • The robot was named "Firefly" because of the new LEDs we experimented with.
  • The LEDs we used to show statuses like aiming, shooting, and picking up cargo.
  • Firefly is our first robot to use swerve drive.
  • Firefly was one of the few robots to use a five ball auto in our week one competition.

Competitions and Awards
  • Lake Superior Regional: Regional Winners, Woodie FLowers Finalist Award (Dahsol Lee)
  • Minnesota 10,000 Lakes Regional: Regional Finalists, Creativity Award
  • FIRST Championships: Quarterfinalists
  • Minnesota State Championship: Finalists

The game starts with a 15 second autonomous period where the robots are completely computer ran.
