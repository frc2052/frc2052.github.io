This game starts off with a 15 second autonomous period where robots are controlled autonomously. Robots may control Boulders, 10 inch wide dodgeballs, and score them in the opponent’s Castle. Each alliance has five defences on their side of the field that change at the beginning of each game. The defence in position one stays the same from game to game, the one in position three changes depending on audience selection, and the remaining three are chosen by the opposing alliance. In Endgame, robots score points by climbing on rungs attached to the opponent’s Castle.
