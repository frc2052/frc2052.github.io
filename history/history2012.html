Team History

2012 Rebound Rumble

Immanis

Robot Information

  • This robot served as a demo robot for longer than any other robot - it was in active duty from 2012 until late 2016.
  • This was the start of our bug/insect themed robots and is named after a spider, Immanis.

Competitions and Awards
  • Lake Superior Regional: Finalists, Engineering Excellence Award
  • Minnesota 10,000 Lakes Regional: Semifinalist, Innovation in Control Award, FIRST Dean's List Finalist Award - Caleb Sykes

This game starts off with a 15 second autonomous period where robots are controlled autonomously. Robots control foam Basketballs and score them in one of four Hoops on the opposite end of the field. In the middle of the field, there is a four inch high Barrier with three Bridges, which are like teeter totters. Robots cross over either one to get to opposite ends of the field.
