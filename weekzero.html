2026 KnightKrawler Week Zero


KnightKrawler has created a wooden version of the field, and we will be running a full day mock competition with it. These matches are intended to provide teams an opportunity to see how their robot interacts with field elements and other robots. Aggressive play (including robot-to-robot contact) is not allowed without prior consent from the other teams in your match. Our week zero events usually allow for the full end-game experience (climbing/balancing). There will be no set match schedule; teams will sign up for play slots on the fly. We will make every effort to give each team equal play time.


We will offer unofficial robot inspections by trained inspectors (including a trained Lead Robot Inspector) and robots will communicate using FMS or an equivalent such as "Freezy Champs", which will require radio configuration at the event. We will also have a number of other trained volunteers present, including a game announcer! There will be no referees at the event, and we will not be tracking scoring.

Your participation in this event is granting permission to have your likeness, photo, and video used in event and team publicity
