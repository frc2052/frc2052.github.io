Welcome to Knightkrawler!

We are Irondale High School's Robotics Team, FRC Team 2052. KnightKrawler is a place where everyone is welcome and students become professionals. We work hard to compete at the highest levels of competition while doing our best to assist all teams and help everyone succeed.

Gracious Professionalism is one of our core values. We work to ensure everyone has success in robotics. How can we help? Contact us and let's work together to build awesome robots and the next generation of engineers and business professionals.
