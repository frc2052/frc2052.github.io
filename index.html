How We're Helping

To support the current need of health care workers, KnightKrawler is running a 3D printing operation to produce face shields.
  • Using 24 printers from 4 schools in our district
  • Operating 10 hours/day, 6 days/week
  • Rate of 1000 shields/week
  • Already delivered to local hospitals including Abott, St. Joe's, Allina, and Children's

How to Run a Shop

If you would like to run a face shield production shop but are in need of 3D printers, we reccomend contacting your local school district. They likely have 3D printers that are going unused if students are learning online right now.

Keeping your volunteers safe:

  • Require volunteers to take their temperature before so they don't come with a fever
  • Everyone should wear a facemask
  • Observe the social distancing regulations of keeping 6 feet apart
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Disinfect keyboards and mice between shifts
  • Only have 3-4 volunteers working at a time (depends on the number of printers you're running)
  • Remember to schedule enough people so you don't have only one student and adult
