KnightKrawler will be competing at the Minne Mini Regional!

Who:

This event is hosted by King Tec, an FRC team from Prior Lake High School. KnightKrawler will be competing along with other Minnesota teams from the area. All KnightKrawler students and their families are invited to attend.

What:

This off-season competition will be very similar to competitions from last year's Destination: Deep Space games. We will use last year's robot, Cricket, to compete. Check out Knightkrawler's top 10 for tips at 2019 competitions!

Where:

Prior Lake High School

7575 150th Street West, Savage, MN 55378

Enter the school through the Main East side doors (lower level).
When:

Saturday, November 23, all day, starting at 8 am. Busing will be provided for all KnightKrawler students to and from the competition. The bus will leave at 7 am from Irondale in the morning.
