Outreach


Previous Next

KnightKrawler is dedicated to engaging with our community through various outreach initiatives. We participate in local events, host workshops, and collaborate with schools to promote STEM education. Our team members volunteer their time to mentor younger students, provide demonstrations of our robots, and inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators. Through these efforts, we aim to foster a love for science and technology and make a positive impact in our community.
