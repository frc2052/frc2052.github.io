Outreach


Previous Next

Team 2052 KnightKrawler is dedicated to engaging with our community through various outreach initiatives. We participate in local events, host workshops, and collaborate with schools to promote STEM education. Our team members volunteer their time to mentor younger students, provide demonstrations of our robots, and inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators. Through these efforts, we aim to foster a love for science and technology and make a positive impact in our community.



Girl Scouts

2052 KnightKrawler partners with local Girl Scout troops to provide hands-on STEM activities and robotics demonstrations. We host workshops where Girl Scouts can learn about engineering principles, programming, and teamwork. Our goal is to inspire young girls to pursue interests in science and technology, and to empower them with the skills and confidence to succeed in these fields. Learn more by visiting our Girl Scouts page!
