KnightKrawler will be competing at the Minnesota Robotics Invitational!

Who:

This event is hosted by the FireBears, an FRC team from Roseville, MN. KnightKrawler will be competing along with other Minnesota teams from the area. All KnightKrawler students and their families are invited to attend.

What:

This off-season competition will be very similar to competitions from last year's Destination: Deep Space games. We will use last year's robot, Cricket, to compete. Check out Knightkrawler's top 10 for tips at 2019 competitions!

In addition, MRI will be livestreamed. Watch the livestream here!
Where:

Roseville Area High School

1240 West County Road B2, Roseville, MN 55113

When:

Saturday, October 12, all day, starting at 8 am.
