KnightKrawler will be competing at the Minnesota 10,000 Lakes Regional Presented by the Medtronic Foundation!

Who:

There will be 63 FRC teams competing at 10K.

Watch the competition from home with the livestream here.

Where:

Williams Arena/The Sports Pavilion Univ of MN
1925 University Avenue SE
Minneapolis, MN USA

What:

The 10,000 Lakes Regional is one of two competitions located in the Twin Cities, occuring alongside the North Star Regional. This Week Five competition uses this years game, Infinite Recharge, and KnightKrawler's 2020 robot, Scorpion. Check out Knightkrawler's Top 10 tips for 2020 competitions!

When:

Thursday, March 26 - Practice Matches all day
Friday, March 27 - Qualification Matches all day Saturday, March 28 - Qualification Matches in the Morning and Elimination Matches in the Afternoon
