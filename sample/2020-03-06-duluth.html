KnightKrawler will be competing at the Northern Lights Regional in Duluth!

Who:

There will be 60 FRC teams competing at Northern Lights.

Watch the competition from home with the livestream here.

Where:

Duluth Entertainment Convention Center (DECC)
Duluth, MN

What:

The Northern Lights Regional is one of two competitions located in Duluth, occuring alongside the Lake Superior Regional. This Week Two competition uses this years game, Infinite Recharge, and KnightKrawler's 2020 robot. Check out Knightkrawler's Top 10 tips for 2020 competitions!

When:

Thursday, March 5 - Practice Matches all day
Friday, March 6 - Qualification Matches all day Saturday, March 7 - Qualification Matches in the Morning and Finals Matches in the Afternoon
