Our Mission

Team 2052 KnightKrawler is Irondale High School's FIRST robotics team. Our mission is to foster future generations of business and STEM leaders via FIRST. As part of our initiative to increase diversity in STEM, we are reaching out to local Girl Scout troops to help them earn their STEM badges and introduce them to FIRST robotics. We hope this resource page can help you learn more about the components of robotics!
