The Roundtable is for Everyone!

Dean's List Candidates

Need something to talk to judges about? Share how you presented to teams from across the FRC community on your area of expertise.

Captains and Student Leaders

Has your team found a process or solution that works well? Is there something you wish worked better? Lead a discussion on how your team operates and ask for feedback from other teams.

Mentors

Looking for advice on choosing captains, drive team members, or other leadership roles? Need help with fundraising or communicating with parents? Join the conversation and ask questions.

New to FRC Robotics?

Build your network. Create relationships. Find friends outside your team. Set yourself and your team up for success through coopertition.

All Students

Looking to grow into a leadership role? Get engaged, expand your knowledge, and establish yourself as a student working to earn a leadership position.

Privacy Policy

The FRC Roundtable honors the spirit of privacy that participants expect at official competitions, such as the FIRST® Championship. The FRC Roundtable is recorded and published. While we encourage participants to use their cameras and contribute to the conversation, we ask that they avoid any undesirable interactions or exposures. Please review our full Privacy Policy
