FRC Roundtable



What is FRC Roundtable?
  • A weekly Sunday night call with FRC teams all over Minnesota
  • Anyone can host a call about any FRC-related topic of their choosing
Why Should I attend?
  • You are able to contribute to the FRC community in Minnesota meaningfully
  • Learn from other teams
  • Have a chance to both ask and answer questions from other teams
Who can Attend?
  • Anyone in FRC who is interested
How can I attend?
  • Join us on Google Meet here: Call link
