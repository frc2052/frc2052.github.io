### 2014: [Aerial Assist](https://youtu.be/oxp4dkMQ1Vo?si=TJJ2Ac0CHE_mu_od)

* Definitions:   
* Goals  
  * Score large exercise balls into goals above and on the ground  
  * Pass the ball between robots  
* Robots  
  * [2175](https://youtu.be/3H1qIbXSpek?t=14)  
  * [195](https://youtu.be/Rb8dNM9HaCY?t=30)  
* Successful Strategies/Designs   
  * Complete control over the exercise ball when passing between robots  
  * The goal is very wide and had a lot of areas that robots could score from, so being able to score consistently from any available position was extremely valuable 

### 2015: [Recycle Rush](https://youtu.be/W6UYFKNGHJ8?si=o8SD1ZSYGz331ZdF)

* Goals  
  * Stack totes up to 6 high  
    * Place Recycling bins on tote stacks  
  * Robots  
    * [3130](https://youtu.be/BWNQSvAM4J8?t=28)  
    * 1678  
  * Successful Strategies/Designs   
    * Elevator that was fast when lifting multiple totes. Numerous teams had elevators that slowed significantly when lifting more than one tote  
    * Tote elevator and recycling bin elevator utilized same super structure or mechanism  
    * Cycles of 5 tote stacks scored more points than cycles of 6 tote stacks

### 2016: [Stronghold](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VqOKzoHJDjA)

* Goals  
  * Score dodgeballs into goals above and on the ground  
    * Cross obstacles (Called defenses) on the field  
    * Climb a bar raised off the ground

    * [5172](https://youtu.be/kqrP6ARKn3Y?t=19)

    * Crossing Defenses with minimal time loss  
    * Score from protected zones

### 2024: Crescendo 

* Definitions  
  * Note: Game piece. 14 in foam ring with an orange coating   
  * Speaker: 10 foot high with an overhang    
  * Amp: Ground level goal. Notes must be scored vertically   
  * Amplification: Scoring 2 notes into the amp allows up to 4 notes scored into the speaker to be worth double points for 10 seconds after the human players presses a button   
  *   
* Goals  
  * Score a foam ring into a goal off the ground   
  * Score 2 foam rings vertically into a goal at ground level to gain a bonus   
  * Climb a horizontal chain   
  * Climb a chain with an additional robot to gain additional points   
  * Score a foam ring in a vertical slot above the chain   
* Robots  
  * 4607  
  * 7028  
  * 3276  
* Successful Strategies/Designs   
  * Scoring 2 notes into the Amp
