# Week Zero Salt Bags

KnightKrawler uses 40lb water softener salt bags to hold down our field perimeter during Week Zero competition. In 2026, KnightKrawer used 1 full pallet (63 bags) of salt. It was loaded directly in the back of a 1 ton pickup truck by forklift. 

## Volunteer Role: Salt Coordinator

### Prior to Friday (1 week out)

* Make a large sign with a photo of a salt bag to place at the merch table.  
* Include map of where to pick up salt (sidewalk on west side of Varsity Gym)  
* “Softner Salt sold at the end of the tournament. We paid $7, your price $4.”  
* Adjust prices each year to suit costs.  
* Find/Create a stand for the sign.  
* Create paper tracking sheet with 63 lines (1 for each salt bag)  
* Retrieve clipboard from shop to hold salt tracking sheet

### Friday (1 day before Week Zero)

* Purchase from Menards to use our tax-exempt account.  
* Arrange for pickup/delivery of salt to Irondale.    
* Ask Scott for QR code to use at checkout to remove sales tax from the purchase  
* After tarps are down, while carpet is being marked, coordinate students to carry in salt  
* 1 salt bag on every low wall support  
* 2 salt bags on all driver station supports

### Saturday Morning (day of Week Zero)

* Place a sign and clipboard at the merch table.  
* Share with merch volunteers how to sell salt  
  * Collect money per bag of salt, place in merch money box  
  * Circle/Highlight/Mark off all the rows for the sale purchased  
  * Write name and phone number next to group of salt  
  * Explain we will call them if they forget to pick up salt at the end of the day

### Saturday Afternoon (day of Week Zero)

* Ask announcers (repeatedly) to share how many bags of salt are left to purchase  
* Retrieve salt sheet from merch table  
* Identify 10 students to carry salt to sidewalk   
* Immediately after tournament ends, carry salt to pickup location  
* Stay with salt until pickup is complete  
* Mark off salt tracking sheet as they are picked up  
* Coordinate with Scott for a place to store any salt not purchased to load and transport
