# Student Packing List for Overnight Competitions

* Gold KnightKrawler t-shirt(s)  
* Maroon workshirt  
* Rally towel  
* Safety glasses (new this year \- only a limited number will be available on site)  
* Clothes for each day (bring layers)  
* Comfortable walking shoes (close-toed)  
* Reusable water bottle (refill stations are on-site at the DECC)  
* Personal care/hygiene items  
* Phones (to communicate with team members/chaperones and keep track of time\!), device chargers, etc. (Slack access is required for travel\!)  
* Phone battery/charge pack  
* Water bottle  
* Bedding is provided on-site for 2 students/bed. Bring a sleeping bag or other bedding supplies if you would like to sleep separately from your roommates.  
* Deodorant  
* Backup Deodorant  
* Swimsuit \- if a pool is available  
* PJs  
* Extra gold shirt if you want to trade with another team

## Winter Destination \- Duluth, MN

* Warm winter clothing items for walking outside (coat, gloves, hat, etc.) 

## Champs

* Something to do on the bus (book, games, notebook & pen)  
* Ear plugs, sleep mask, portable pillow for sleeping on the bus  
* Change of clothes for bus ride back  
* Small bag/backpack for on the bus items  
* Sunglasses & Sunscreen

## Money

* Breakfast is typically provided by the hotel or team  
* Lunch $10-15  per day  
* Dinner $15-20 per day  
* Souvenir shirts are available   
* Debit card, Credit card, or reloadable cash card (Some venues are becoming cashless)

## Medications

* OTC medicines (Advil, Allergy, Lactaid)  
* Check with team advisor on updated advice for managing prescriptions at an event

## Do No Bring

* Games to the venue  
* Stuff to the venue you do not plan to carry all day  
* Unnecessary valuables and extra cash
