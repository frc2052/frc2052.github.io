# Season Outline and Leadership Reminders

The following is a rough list of things that happen each month of a robotics season to help leaders plan ahead. Included is a list of specific tasks that should be completed in those months.

## June

* Summer FRC Picnic  
* Leadership Transition Meeting  
* Farmers Market  
* Clean Shop  
* Bike Tune Up Event  
* To Do List  
  * Register for Parades  
  * Organize Summer Outreach  
  * Organize Summer Social Meetups  
  * Training Planning

## July

* Parades  
* Farmers Market  
* To Do List  
  * Register for Off-season competitions in fall  
  * Training Planning

## August

* Budget Meeting  
* To Do List  
  * Training Planning  
  * Finalize Info Night Date (new student registration)  
  * Reserve Compost Site (last week of August)  
  * File Taxes  (Fiscal Year Ends Jun 30\)  
  * Finalize Fall Training Schedule

## September

* Info Night Presentation (2 weeks after School Starts)  
* First Meeting \- Name games \- Intro to sub-teams  
* 2nd-4th Meeting \- Sub team Training  
* Optional Meeting \- Driver practice for offseason events  
* To Do List  
  * Check on FTC Regional needs/volunteers  
  * Register Team for season  
  * Info Night Posters  
  * Finalize Info Night Presentation

## October

* First week \- Finish into level training  
* MinneTrails KickOff (second week of Oct) \- whole team  
* 2nd meeting after MT Kickoff \- Brain storming Mechanisms \- whole team  
* 3rd meeting after MT Kickoff \- CAD, Paper, Whiteboard Mechanism Design \- whole team  
* 4th meeting after MT Kickoff \- Design review \- Decide what to prototype \- whole team  
* 5th meeting after MT Kickoff \- Subteams Split \- Build: Prototyping, Programming Training/Drive code, Media: Training & Reveal Videos  
* To Do List  
  * Register for Regular Season Competitions  
  * Register to be KOP Pickup Site  
  * Open Week Zero Registration  
  * Reserve Hotel for Regional 1  
  * Individual Team Photos & Uniform Sizing  
  * Hold meeting on Core Values and Team History  
  * Contact Sponsors  
  * Check on FTC Tournament needs with High Tech Kids

## November

* Optional Meeting(s) \- driver practice for second regional  
* Build: MinneTrials robots  
* Programming: Complete training tasks, program MinneTrials Robots  
* Media: MinneTrials Reveal Videos, continued Training  
* Off season competition  
* To Do List  
  * Online Merch Ordering  
  * Reserve Hotel for Regional 1

## December

* MinneTrials competition  
* Wrap up fall training  
* Remove electronics/swerve from old robot if not keeping for demos  
* Create prototype chassis for programming  
* Parent Meeting for Week Zero Volunteers  
* Mock Kick-off  
* Clean Shop  
* Clean Week Zero storage  
* Disassemble Field Elements  
* To Do List  
  * Invite Alumni to Kickoff  
  * Coordinate build season lunches  
  * Finalize Sponsor List for Uniforms  
  * Start making Sponsor Patches  
  * Coordinate for delivery of KoP bins

## January

* Kickoff  
* Design Team (Sunday after Kickoff) \- Settle on prototypes to design Week 1  
* Week 1 \- Design \- Wood prototypes \- Programmers start auto paths  
* Week 2 \- Wood Prototypes \- CAD initial designs \- Programmers start subsystems and commands  
* Week 3 \- Mechanisms start final build \- Programmers complete initial subsystems and commands  
* Week 4 \- First Mechanisms complete \- Build/Programming testing 

## February

* Week 5 \- All Mechanisms complete \- Build testing  
* Week 6 \- Autonomous Testing \- Create spare parts  
* Week 7 \- Week Zero  
* Parent travel meeting  
* Reveal Night  
* Team Photo  
* To Do List  
  * Create Week Zero volunteer list/shifts  
  * Find truck to pull trailer for Regional 1  
  * Get quotes for Buses to Champs  
  * Buy Sponsor Banner  
  * Collect uniforms for sleeve patch sewing
